Hi, I'm Pakhi

I build AI agents with something like hunger, rest, and a social life. The idea is simple: an agent whose behavior comes from what it needs is easier to understand, and to trust, than one patched into behaving after the fact. That question, safety by design rather than by afterthought, runs through everything here: homeostatic RL, intrinsic motivation, and now social drives in multi-agent systems.

Currently a research intern at Aalto University. MSc student at TU Dresden.
